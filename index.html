About This Project

Our Mission

This study hub was created to support Sol Plaatje University students in the Natural and Applied Sciences faculty, specifically those studying Information Systems III, Data Science, and Computer Science. Our goal is to provide comprehensive, well-organized study materials that complement classroom learning and help students excel in their studies.

All notes are carefully curated to highlight the most important concepts from each module, with interactive elements like quizzes and flashcards to enhance learning and retention.

About the Creator

This study tool was created by Johannes Ethusang, a 3rd year ICT student at Sol Plaatje University passionate about creating educational resources that make learning more accessible and effective.

The project is open source and available on GitHub, where contributions and suggestions are welcome from both students and educators.
